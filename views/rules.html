Rules :

To win this game, you must sheer all of your enemy shepard sheeps. To achieve this mission, some weapons are available :

  • Sheers : The basic weapon, sheer the target point;
  • Strimmer : Only once during the game, cut the 9 cases near to the target point;
  • Baldness epidemic : Only once during the game, Sheer 5 cases near to the target point;
  • Unknown : Only once during the game, Sheer a whole flock if it contains only 2 sheeps or less.
